Fun Facts About the Super Smash Bros. Series

  • While in development, the game was referred to as "Pepsiman."
  • The creator of Super Smash Bros., Masahiro Sakurai, voiced King Dedede.
  • Before Super Smash Bros. Brawl's release, a website called "Smash Dojo" was created, which gave new information about the game every day until release.
  • Super Smash Bros. is regarded as the biggest gaming crossover of all time.
  • The soundtrack for Super Smash Bros. Ultimate contains over 24 hours of music content.
  • Smash Bros. has sold over 40,000,000 units worldwide as a franchise.
  • This series represents over 40 different game series' within it.
  • Super Smash Bros. Brawl was the first Smash game to cut characters from its roster.
  • Super Smash Bros. Brawl introduced the Final Smash mechanic.
  • Smash Brawl was the first game to incorporate third-party franchises into the series, adding Sonic the Hedgehog and Solid Snake into the game.
  • Ultimate contains the largest roster out of all the Smash games, and contains everyone from the previous games as well as many newcomers to this game.
  • Super Smash Bros. was originally called "Kakuto-Geemu Ryuoh" or "Dragon King: The Fighting Game." This would have become an entirely different fighting game altogether had it gone through, and likely would not have seen the massive success that Smash Bros. saw as a series.
  • The top 10 most popular Smash YouTuber's in order of least popular to most popular are: NairoMK, Beefy Smash Doods, Mr Danish Butter Cookie, ESAM, YEET Smash, IntroSpecktive, PJiggles, Hopcat, Alpharad, and Little Z.
